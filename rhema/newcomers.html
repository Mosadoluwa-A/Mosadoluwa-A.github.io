A typical sunday

The Sunday service usually starts with an intense prayer session to charge the atmosphere. This then followed by a very enjoyable and soul lifting praise and worship session. Then, we have the congregational prayer and then get entertained by the beautiful special rendition of the choir (yes it is always beautiful). After we share the word and then take announcements.

A brief description to the church location in case you are having issues:

From mobil filling station, go straight down towards the Zenith Bank and then turn to your right and go uphill. Continue on a straight line until you reach the bridge, you would already see the auditorium and the sign board from there.
