Join us on Sundays

We seek the face of the Lord and fellowship together with one another in two powerful services

The services are streamed via our facebook platform so you can join us between 8 and 10am for our first service and between 10 and 12pm for our second service. You can also view our previous services and real time commentary.

We also prioritise good music and try as much as possible to have an enjoyable praise and worship session with a good sound system too. We do not have any particular dresscode so you can feel free to come in your favorite clothes.
