Leprosy is Curable!

People affected with Leprosy (PALs) Do not deserved to be ostracized and treated like outcasts. Join us in helping to make their lives better and reintegrating back into society after cure.

What we Do

Damien Foundation (Nigeria Project) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that is focused and dedicated to the eradication of Leprosy and Tuberculosis in Nigeria and the world at large. We aim to at least reduce it to the barest minimum and assists victims with recovery.
